Players Gear

Gum shields

Gum shields are used to protect the player teeth and mouths from potential damage in a collision.

Boots

Rugby boots are a key aspect to players performance. As rugby is played in all weather types and is a contact sport, rugby boots are used to provide extra traction to players. Especially when positioned in the forwards.

Head gear

Head gear such a scrum caps can be used to provide protection from cuts and potential bruising in some cases. A common condition player use scrum caps to prevent is cauliflower ears.

Equipment

Ball

Without the ball there's no game. these can vary in size to suit the age group of the players.

Post protectors

As the rugby posts are position on the try line, post protectors are used to protect the player form injury when colliding with the posts.

Flags

Flags are placed around the pitch to mark the try lines, halfway lines and the 25m lines respectively. These make it easier to identify which area the ball or a player was in when they crossed the touch line.
