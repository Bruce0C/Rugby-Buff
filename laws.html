Sportmanship and Ethos

Rugby is a game that expects players to have integrity and honesty. At all levels of the sports , discipline, passion, solidarity and respect are key elements that make the sport enjoyable to play and to watch. Along with these core values players are also expected to play within the current laws and regulations.
